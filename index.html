About Us

The network of specialized stores "In vino" is more than 1500 names of wines and spirits, drinks of famous brands, exclusives and new items at very attractive prices, this is a wide choice and an individual approach to customers. 20 stores are already open across the country. “In vino” is more than just a good choice, it is a philosophy of wine that has absorbed the traditions of making, storing and consuming great drinks. It is not enough to buy wine, it is important to enjoy it, to discover something new for yourself, so in our stores sommeliers and cavists will help you choose the right drink for a certain moment or event.

Tastings

For the comfort and convenience of visitors, in some of the shops "In vino" there are bars where you can taste the drink that you want to buy to make sure you have made the right choice. In InVino, the entourage and drinks very well "complement" each other. The author's interior, dim light, background music, light as a sip of wine, European cuisine, whose dishes are designed to complement and reveal the taste of wine - everything is done here for a pleasant, relaxed, excellent and memorable pastime. Lovers and connoisseurs of the wonderful world of fine drinks are welcome at InVino every day from 11 to 24 hours.
